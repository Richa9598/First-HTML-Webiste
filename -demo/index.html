NUPUR SAJ - FOUNDER



Neha Rajesh Patel

(Founder Of Nupur Saj)



I started this school in 1998 after completing my masters program in Bharatnatyam. I am a trained Bharatnatyam dancer and have a great interests in the local and cultural folk dances of India. It was my dream to teach the rich and beautiful dance culture of our country to all the childrens. My main aim is to maintain the dignity and value of Indian culture and heridity which is on the way to vanish due to the impact of western culture on the youth. My wish is to reach out to all the childerns and aults who are willing to learn indian dance and folk dance.

WeAllDance

It is an organization founded by Neha Rajesh Patel. The aim of this project is to make dance available to each and every child who is willing to learn do not have enough money to pay his/her class fees. In this program various activities are held in which workshops are arranged, competitions are arranged for childrens.
